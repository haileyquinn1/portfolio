Food Findr App Idea: Our Mission and Vision

    App Description

    College is hard, and it becomes even harder when you're broke and hungry. With FoodFindr, we aim to make the hunt for FREE food easy and fun, and help you find some interesting events around your college campus. FoodFindr allows you to use your current location, or select the college you attend, to bring up a map of where free food can be found around campus. Our goal for this app was for it to be one part menu, and one part community, with an overarching mission to reduce unnecessary food waste on college campuses.
