ChefMates - App Idea

  • Project Description

    This year, in my interaction design studio class, we were put into teams and tasked with creating a product that tried to do some good for the world. In light of the COVID-19 pandemic forcing many people into quarantine and unable to see their friends and family, we noticed that people were feeling more disconnected than ever. We wanted to create an application that allowed passionate cooks and food lovers to be able to connect with one another at a time where many are at cooking at home, or looking to pick up the hobby.
