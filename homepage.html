  • FoodFindr App Idea

    College is hard, and it becomes even harder when you're broke and hungry. With FoodFindr, we aim to make the hunt for FREE food easy and fun, and help you find some interesting events around your college campus. FoodFindr allows you to use your current location, or select the college you attend, to bring up a map of where free food can be found around campus
